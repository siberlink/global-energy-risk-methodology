# Why Energy Prices Fail to Capture Risk

Energy prices reflect **current market equilibrium**, not underlying risk.

## Key Limitations

- Lagging response to geopolitical events  
- Overreaction to short-term sentiment  
- Underpricing tail risks  
- Ignoring structural system stress  

## Example

Markets can remain stable while:

- Conflict intensity rises  
- Supply routes become vulnerable  
- Storage buffers decline  

This creates a **false sense of stability**.

---

## Conclusion

Risk must be measured independently from price.

EnergyRiskIQ indices are designed to capture this hidden layer.

# Europe LNG Supply & Demand — Market Structure and Risk Analysis

The European LNG market plays a critical role in balancing gas supply, especially during periods of geopolitical disruption.

This page explains how LNG flows impact **energy security, pricing, and system resilience**.

---

## Why LNG Matters for Europe

Europe relies heavily on LNG to:

- Replace pipeline gas disruptions  
- Stabilize supply during peak demand  
- Maintain storage refill targets  

This makes LNG a **key risk transmission channel**.

---

## Key Risk Factors

- Global LNG competition (Asia vs Europe)  
- Shipping constraints and freight costs  
- Terminal capacity limitations  
- Supply concentration risk  

---

## System Impact

Changes in LNG flows directly affect:

- Gas prices (TTF)  
- Storage refill pace  
- Energy security outlook

# Global Energy Risk Forecast — Forward-Looking Risk Modeling

Energy markets are driven by expectations.

The **Global Energy Risk Forecast** provides a forward-looking view of how risk may evolve over the next 24–72 hours.

---

## What This Forecast Measures

- Short-term geopolitical escalation risk  
- Probability of supply disruption events  
- Market-sensitive risk signals  
- Directional pressure on energy systems  

---

## Why Forecasting Risk Matters

Markets move based on:

→ Expected disruption  
→ Perceived escalation  
→ Anticipated constraints  

Not just current conditions.

---

## How to Use It

- Identify early risk shifts  
- Anticipate volatility  
- Align trading strategies  
- Monitor escalation dynamics

# European Gas Storage Levels — System Risk Indicator

Gas storage is one of the most critical indicators of **energy system resilience in Europe**.

This page explains how storage levels impact risk, pricing, and market stability.

---

## Why Storage Matters

Storage acts as:

- A buffer against supply disruptions  
- A stabilizer for seasonal demand  
- A key signal of system health  

---

## Risk Factors

- Low storage entering winter  
- Slow refill pace  
- High dependency on LNG  
- Weather-driven demand spikes  

---

## Market Impact

Storage levels influence:

- Gas prices (TTF)  
- Volatility  
- Risk perception  
- Policy decisions

---

## Key Insight

Low storage does not always move prices immediately.

But it **raises system fragility**, increasing the probability of future shocks.

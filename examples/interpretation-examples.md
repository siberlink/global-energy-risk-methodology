# Interpretation Examples

## Example 1

GERI rising + prices flat  
→ Market underpricing geopolitical risk  

## Example 2

EERI rising faster than GERI  
→ Europe-specific escalation risk  

## Example 3

EGSI elevated + storage low  
→ Structural gas system vulnerability

## Key Insight

Indices are **leading indicators**, not lagging confirmations.

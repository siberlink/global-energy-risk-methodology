# Scoring Framework

All indices are normalized on a 0–100 scale.

## Risk Bands

| Range | Level |
|---|---|
| 0–20 | Low |
| 21–40 | Elevated |
| 41–60 | High |
| 61–80 | Severe |
| 81–100 | Critical |

---

## Core Components

Indices are constructed from weighted components:

- Event severity (war, sanctions, disruptions)
- Geographic impact
- Energy system exposure
- Market sensitivity
- Persistence of risk signals

---

## Interpretation

- Rising index → increasing systemic risk  
- Falling index → risk normalization  
- Divergence vs prices → sentiment disconnect
